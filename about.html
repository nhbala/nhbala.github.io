About Me

Hi! I'm Nathan Bala, a current Senior at Cornell University studying Computer Science. I'm passionate about creating technologies that positively impact the people and communities around me. I previously have interned at Capital One working on bank/consumer messages and metrics and currently serve as a Software Subteam Lead for AguaClara Cornell, a project team that helps create sustainable water filtration plants for communities in Honduras. This upcoming summer I will be in Seattle working as a Software Engineering Intern at Microsoft before returning back to Cornell to finish my Masters.

Outside of Computer Science, I am currently pursuing a minor in Asian American Studies and have been a member of the Cornell Symphony Orchestra. I also enjoy Korean BBQ and attempting to become a somewhat passable cook.
