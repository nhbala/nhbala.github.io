Languages/Frameworks Used: Python

"Remain Silent"

Previous Next

Languages/Frameworks Used: Javascript, HTML, CSS

"Remain Silent" is a data visulization that seeks to illustrates United States civilians' deaths at the hands of the American Police from 2015 to 2019. Filters regarding how the individual was killed, if they were armed/fleeing, demographic of location where the killing took place, and ethnicity are present to allow users to find view specific subsets of the data and draw their on conclusion on the patterns present. A second visulization is also presented to allow users to view the specific locations where these killings have taken place and filter these out by specific years.
